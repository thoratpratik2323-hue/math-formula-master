∫ Calculus Formulae

dx
Standard Derivatives
▼

Power Rule & Exponentials

$$ \frac{d}{dx} (x^n) = n x^{n-1} $$ $$ \frac{d}{dx} (e^x) = e^x $$ $$ \frac{d}{dx} (a^x) = a^x \ln(a) $$ $$ \frac{d}{dx} (\ln x) = \frac{1}{x} $$

Trigonometric Functions

$$ \frac{d}{dx} (\sin x) = \cos x $$ $$ \frac{d}{dx} (\cos x) = -\sin x $$ $$ \frac{d}{dx} (\tan x) = \sec^2 x $$ $$ \frac{d}{dx} (\sec x) = \sec x \tan x $$

Inverse Trigonometric

$$ \frac{d}{dx} (\sin^{-1} x) = \frac{1}{\sqrt{1 - x^2}} $$ $$ \frac{d}{dx} (\tan^{-1} x) = \frac{1}{1 + x^2} $$ $$ \frac{d}{dx} (\sec^{-1} x) = \frac{1}{|x|\sqrt{x^2 - 1}} $$

Rules of Differentiation

Product: $$ (uv)' = u'v + uv' $$ Quotient: $$ \left(\frac{u}{v}\right)' = \frac{u'v - uv'}{v^2} $$ Chain: $$ \frac{dy}{dx} = \frac{dy}{du} \cdot \frac{du}{dx} $$

Standard Integrals
▼

Basic Integrals

$$ \int x^n \, dx = \frac{x^{n+1}}{n+1} + C \quad (n \neq -1)$$ $$ \int \frac{1}{x} \, dx = \ln|x| + C $$ $$ \int e^x \, dx = e^x + C $$ $$ \int a^x \, dx = \frac{a^x}{\ln a} + C $$

Trigonometric Integrals

$$ \int \sin x \, dx = -\cos x + C $$ $$ \int \cos x \, dx = \sin x + C $$ $$ \int \tan x \, dx = \ln|\sec x| + C $$ $$ \int \sec^2 x \, dx = \tan x + C $$

Special Forms

$$ \int \frac{dx}{x^2 + a^2} = \frac{1}{a} \tan^{-1}\left(\frac{x}{a}\right) + C $$ $$ \int \frac{dx}{\sqrt{a^2 - x^2}} = \sin^{-1}\left(\frac{x}{a}\right) + C $$ $$ \int e^x [f(x) + f'(x)] \, dx = e^x f(x) + C $$

Integration By Parts (ILATE)

$$ \int u \, v \, dx = u \int v \, dx - \int \left( u' \int v \, dx \right) dx $$

📊 Algebra Formulae

M
Matrices & Determinants
▼

Determinant Properties

$$ |AB| = |A||B| $$ $$ |A^T| = |A| $$ $$ |kA| = k^n |A| $$ (where n is order of matrix)

Adjoint & Inverse

$$ A^{-1} = \frac{1}{|A|} \text{adj}(A) $$ $$ A \cdot \text{adj}(A) = |A|I $$ $$ |\text{adj}(A)| = |A|^{n-1} $$

🧊 Vectors & 3D Geometry

v
Vector Algebra
▼

Dot (Scalar) Product

$$ \vec{a} \cdot \vec{b} = |\vec{a}| |\vec{b}| \cos\theta $$ $$ \vec{a} \cdot \vec{b} = a_1b_1 + a_2b_2 + a_3b_3 $$ If perpendicular: $$ \vec{a} \cdot \vec{b} = 0 $$

Cross (Vector) Product

$$ |\vec{a} \times \vec{b}| = |\vec{a}| |\vec{b}| \sin\theta $$ Area of triangle = $$ \frac{1}{2} |\vec{a} \times \vec{b}| $$ Area of ||gm = $$ |\vec{a} \times \vec{b}| $$

Projection

Projection of $\vec{a}$ on $\vec{b}$: $$ \frac{\vec{a} \cdot \vec{b}}{|\vec{b}|} $$
